The Build Roadmap

How We Raise Frames

We orchestrate clean, professional, and rapid carpentry development phases for standard and custom timber structures.

01

Planing & Specs

We carefully inspect your architect's framing layout plans, load paths, lumber requirements, and detail specifications.

02

Lumber Pre-Cut

Timber sections are measured, calculated, and high-precision pre-cut to secure absolutely tight joinery joints.

03

Frame Raising

Our framing crew coordinates on the site, standing and securing timber studs, lintels, joists, and top plates.

04

Cladding & Joists

Rafter configurations and architectural wood cladding panels are wrapped and installed, yielding a weather-tight house skeleton.
